Welcome to Helping Hands

About Our Organisation

Every year in South Africa, over 20,000 animals are abandoned, injured, or unhealthy. Helping Hands Rescue Animal South Africa was founded by Athol Fenderick in October 2009 to provide shelter, nutrition, and care. Through fundraising and community events, we raise awareness about animal cruelty and support no-kill shelters.

"The real heroes are the animals that have been hurt but continue to love us unconditionally."

Join the conversation on all social media platforms about changing the lives of our pets and best friends.

Quick Statistic

87% of animals rescued by Helping Hands find their “furever” homes within the first 6 months of rescue!
